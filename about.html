About the game:

Welcome to Donut Maker, a deliciously addictive game that takes inspiration from the legendary Cookie Clickers! Just like its predecessor, Donut Maker revolves around the simple concept of clicking a mouthwatering treat to accumulate an ever-growing collection. Prepare to be captivated by the satisfying sensation of generating an abundance of donuts, as you embark on an exciting journey filled with sprinkles, glazes, and endless cravings.


About us:

Fred the Baker Baking Company is a haven for donut enthusiasts seeking unforgettable taste experiences. Our dedicated team of skilled bakers and pastry chefs meticulously handcraft each donut, combining traditional recipes with new flavors and techniques. With our commitment to using only the finest quality ingredients, every bite of our indulgent treats is a moment of pure delight. Whether it's a morning pick-me-up, an afternoon treat, or a late-night indulgence, Fred the Baker Baking Company is here to create sweet memories and satisfy your cravings, one donut at a time.
